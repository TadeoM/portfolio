Overview

HIT 'EM is a game that plays like pong, with a twist. You first must get through obstacles that take multiple hits to destroy, otherwise it will be very difficult to score. Also did I mention that the longer you play without scoring, the more balls that will spawn?

I'm hoping to add a little simulated screen here, where there are a bunch of obstacles and some balls that fly around the screen.
