Dealz Simulatorr

Watch videos and make money!

Role: Developer

In Dealz Simulator, we wanted to make a simple game, as this was the first team project we had ever done, and the other two members of the team had never worked in Unity. We decided on this, a idler game where you buy phones to watch videos that make money for you. Aside from buying phones you can hire friends to help you make even more more money over time.

My part of this project was phone behavior and designing the statitistics that they would have. Phones had chances of crashing and blowing up and removing a slot from the board, meaning the player loses a maximum phone number, and I knew things such as, the iPhone should have the least chances of exploding and crashing, but to balance it, the iPhone also made the least amount of money, meanwhile, the Samsung Galaxy 7 had the highest risk of exploding, but made the most money.
