About Me

Hello! I am Tadeo Menichelli, I was born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, and currently live in Rochester, New York. I studied Game Design & Development at the Rochester Institute of Technology and have a minor in Creative Writing. I have a passion for Gameplay Programming, and am very curious about learning to program game AI. My favorite game setting is Warcraft. I spent as much time as I could playing Warcraft 2 and 3 as a kid, and have played a ton of World of Warcraft. The strategy genre is my favorite and I'd put Total War Warhammer 1 & 2 at close second favorite game, Warcraft being the first. Outside of digital games, I am trying to start Rock Climbing. I also love to play Guitar, and have played alto and baritone saxophone throughout most of my highschool career. During the winter you can even find me snowboarding whenever I can get up to the mountains.
